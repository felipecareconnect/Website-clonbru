The Origin

Why Clonbru exists

Clonbru was born from a simple frustration. Walking the streets of Dublin, speaking with restaurant owners, shop managers and service providers, the same story kept coming up: great businesses, loyal customers, skilled teams — but almost invisible online.

Meanwhile, their competitors — not necessarily better, just better optimised — were picking up every new customer who turned to Google. The gap wasn't about quality of service. It was about who showed up first.

That's the problem Clonbru was built to solve. We focus on the channels that matter most for local visibility: Google Business Profile, Local SEO and Google Ads — because that's where your next customer is searching right now.

We're based in Dublin. We serve Ireland. And we're obsessed with one thing: making sure the businesses that deserve to be found actually are.
